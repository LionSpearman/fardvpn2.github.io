Profile.

Needs

The SAKADAB institution is struggling with the lack of awareness of their institution and their businesses. This is due to the lack of skills and knowledge in mediums such as social media and has led to other issues such as inconsistent income flow.

Project Proposal

Using our available resources, we have come up with a two-phase project proposal to help them acquire the needed skills and platforms to advertise their institution and business in form of websites, social media posts, and online campaigns.

Labor

Group Member's skills that are manifested through our aptitudes in photography, instruments, graphic design and coding.

Capital

We own devices and equipment that are
• Manifested through our DSLR camera, Electric Guitar, PC's, Tablets, and more.
• Software

Objectives

•To inspire, help and train other people with the same limitations as them
•To show people that despite their disabilities, they are just as capable and talented as any other able-bodied person.
•Are able to live and sustain themselves without help.
•Aim to support each other as well as others with the same situation as them.
•Enhance their community in all aspects.

Factors for Creation

•Lack of freedom to do anything independently.
•Lack of accessibility

Advocacy

•They implement public fundraisers like concerts for the public to watch.
•They also have a Facebook page where visitors can learn and reach them.
•Every person must share equal contribution in their community.
•They each have a job or role to do to support their institution.



Impact

As an online campaign, it has a very low cost and resource need other than the Wi-Fi or Mobile data needed to post the advertisements and the time and effort needed to learn the skills.

Potential

Through this Project Proposal, it can help bring more traction to their business and bringing a wider pool of customers and making the institution more well-known in general. This will make their income flow more stable and reliable, helping solve their issue of money scarcity.

Sustainability

It is sustainable because what we offer is not something fully tangible and material but a skill that can stay and grow with them even long after we leave the institution. By giving them designs and templates we give them examples of how to make a good advertisement and if ever they are confused, they can refer to our tutorial videos to refresh their memories.
To ensure sustainability however, we will also be teaching them the sources of the information and good learning material on how they can further their skills on their own.

Goods Offered

  • Layouts and designs for social media posters and advertisements
  • A website that the institution can customize

Services Offered

  • Tutorial sessions in formats like Google Meet, Zoom, or tutorial videos
  • Training on how to use digital applications and social media like Canva, Facebook, and Instagram
  • Skills development for maintaining their own accounts and website, such as coding, graphic design, photography, and music
